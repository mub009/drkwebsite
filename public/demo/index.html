Our FAQS

Questions? We're Glad you asked

Quick answer to questions you have. Can't find what you're looking for?

How can I book an appointment?

You can book online through our website, call our 24/7 helpline, or visit the hospital in person. Same‑day appointments may be available depending on department and availability.

What should I bring to my first visit?

Please bring a valid ID, insurance card, list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or test results from previous visits.

Do you offer telemedicine or virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations for many specialties. Contact us to schedule a telemedicine appointment and receive care from the comfort of your home.

Is emergency care available?

Yes, our emergency department is open 24/7 with board-certified emergency physicians and state-of-the-art equipment to handle all types of medical emergencies.

How do I get the results of my lab tests or imaging studies?

Test results are available through our patient portal within 24-48 hours. You'll receive an email notification when results are ready, or you can call our results hotline.
