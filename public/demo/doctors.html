Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery

Oral and maxillofacial surgery is a branch of dentistry that focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of diseases, deformities, and injuries affecting the soft and hard tissues of the mouth, jaws, and maxillofacial structures and fillings. This specialty encompasses both functional and cosmetic aspects. Maxillofacial surgery procedures range from simple to complex.

Clinic Services

  • Cosmetic silicone implants for the face and jaw corners
  • Maxillofacial deformities
  • Lip shortening
  • Gummy smile treatment
  • Prominent ear and bat ear correction
  • Eyelid and eyebrow plastic surgery
  • Treatment of sleep apnea due to jaw recession
  • Facial cosmetic surgeries
  • Jaw and chin surgeries
  • Cheek and neck liposuction
  • Diagnosis and treatment of facial pain
  • Partial and full facelift

Reconstructive, Plastic and Burn Surgery

Dr. Khalid Al Rahimi Hospital boasts exceptional expertise in reconstructive and cosmetic surgeries, keeping pace with the latest scientific techniques in the field. The plastic surgery clinic has a warm and friendly atmosphere, making it an ideal environment to meet each patient’s individual needs. Patients receive treatment from the best plastic surgeons, who utilize the latest scientific advances in the field. At the center, we believe in listening to your needs and offering our full expertise. We provide comprehensive consultations about your treatment options and help you make the most appropriate decision. All of these services, and more, are provided in a secure and confidential environment.

Services Offered

  • Cosmetic surgery after bariatric surgery
  • Body shaping (upper and lower body lifts, arm lifts, thigh lifts)
  • Breast surgery (reduction, augmentation or lift)
  • Tighten the abdominal area
  • Rhinoplasty
  • Facelift
  • Blepharoplasty
  • Otoplasty (correction of prominent ears)
  • Liposuction using Vaser technology for body shaping
  • Fat and stem cell injections to improve the appearance of the face, breasts, or buttocks and restore youthfulness
  • Non-surgical interventions
  • Breast reconstruction using tissue taken from the patient or implants
  • Microscopic reconstructive surgeries
  • Correction of congenital defects (such as cleft lip, vascular malformations, birthmarks)
  • Dermatological surgery (birthmarks, pigmentation, skin tumors, scars, and blemishes resulting from injuries)
  • Treatment of soft tissue swelling (such as lipoma, sebaceous cyst, and synovial cyst)
  • Treatment of burns (up to 20% of the total body surface area)
  • Relieving skin contractures and fibrosis caused by burns
  • Removing burn scars using balloon technology
  • Non-surgical interventions to improve the quality of old scars
  • Fixation of hand bone fractures
  • Tendon injury surgeries, from simple to tendon transplantation
  • Treatment of peripheral nerve syndromes

Rays

The Department of Medical Imaging and Radiology at Dr. Khalid Al Rahimi Hospital is committed to serving patients with the latest technological advancements and innovative treatment options. Under the hospital's umbrella, we bring together a group of radiology specialists with extensive experience in their field, in addition to the latest equipment in our outpatient clinics. Services Offered

  • X‑ray & digital radiography (including fluoroscopy)
  • Ultrasound & colour Doppler (including fetal, vascular, small‑parts, elastography)

  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI), including advanced protocols and functional imaging

  • Pediatric, emergency, oncologic, cardiac & neuro imaging services
  • Picture Archiving and Communication System (PACS) & Radiology Information System (RIS) support

Intensive Care Unit

Dr. Khaled Al Rahimi Hospital houses a state‑of‑the‑art intensive care unit (ICU), designed to accommodate a variety of medical conditions. The unit is located directly adjacent to other relevant departments. Intensivists provide the best healthcare to patients using the latest technology, including advanced systems to ensure a safe environment and close patient monitoring.

Services Offered

  • Advanced continuous vital signs & organ-function monitoring
  • Mechanical ventilation (invasive & non‑invasive), sedation & pain management
  • Hemodynamic & cardiovascular support (infusion pumps, pacing, ECMO options)
  • Renal support: dialysis & continuous renal replacement therapy (CRRT)
  • Enteral & parenteral nutrition management
  • Portable imaging & diagnostics: bedside ultrasound, X‑ray, CT, blood‐gas & point‑of‑care labs
  • Procedures: intubation, central/arterial line insertion, thoracentesis, bronchoscopy, peritoneal lavage
  • Management of sepsis, multi‑organ failure, severe trauma, respiratory failure, neurological emergencies
  • Multidisciplinary care: intensivists, ICU nurses, pharmacists, respiratory therapists, dietitians & rehab staff
  • Family support: regular updates, emotional counseling, including psychological services
  • Strict infection control & quality-improvement protocols
